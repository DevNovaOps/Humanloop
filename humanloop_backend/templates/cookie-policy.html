Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and improve your experience. HumanLoop uses a minimal number of cookies, primarily for essential functionality.

Cookies We Use

Below is a detailed list of cookies used by HumanLoop:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
sessionid Maintains your login session so you stay authenticated 2 weeks Essential
csrftoken Protects against Cross-Site Request Forgery attacks 1 year Essential
humanloop_theme Remembers your preferred theme (light/dark/system) 1 year Preference

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are strictly necessary for the Platform to function. Without these cookies, you would not be able to log in or use the Platform securely. These cookies:

  • Do not collect personal information for marketing purposes
  • Are automatically deleted when their expiry period ends
  • Cannot be disabled as they are required for basic functionality
  • Are first-party cookies (set by HumanLoop only)

Preference Cookies

Preference cookies remember your settings and choices to provide a more personalized experience. These include your theme preference (light/dark mode). You can clear these cookies at any time through your browser settings without affecting platform functionality.

Third-Party Cookies

HumanLoop does not use any third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We do not share cookie data with advertisers or marketing networks. The only external resources loaded are Google Fonts for typography, which do not set cookies on your device.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View all cookies stored on your device
  • Delete individual or all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific or all websites
  • Set your browser to notify you when a cookie is set

Note: Blocking essential cookies will prevent you from logging into HumanLoop. We recommend keeping essential cookies enabled for the best experience.

Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information on our cookie practices.
