Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

Introduction

HumanLoop ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Social Innovation Platform. By using HumanLoop, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, mobile number, organization name, role, and date of birth provided during registration.
  • Profile Data: Language preferences, profile settings, and notification preferences.
  • Usage Data: Log data, pilot/program participation details, feedback submissions, expense records, and activity logs.
  • Device Data: Browser type, IP address, and device identifiers for security and analytics purposes.
  • Cookies: Essential session cookies required for authentication. See our Cookie Policy for details.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the HumanLoop platform services
  • To authenticate users and manage session security
  • To connect beneficiaries with relevant social innovation programs
  • To generate AI-powered insights and recommendations for program improvement
  • To issue certificates upon program completion
  • To communicate platform updates, notifications, and service-related information
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your information may be shared in the following limited cases:

  • With NGOs: If you are enrolled in a pilot program, the assigned NGO may see your enrollment details to facilitate program delivery.
  • With Administrators: Platform administrators can access user information for platform management and support.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Service Providers: We may use third-party services for analytics and infrastructure, all bound by confidentiality agreements.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted password storage using Django's PBKDF2 hashing
  • CSRF protection on all form submissions
  • Session-based authentication with secure cookie settings
  • Regular security audits and access logging
  • Role-based access control (RBAC) to restrict data visibility

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can download all your data at any time from Settings → Privacy → Download My Data.
  • Correction: You can update your profile information from the Settings page.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account from Settings → Security → Danger Zone.
  • Data Portability: Your data is available for download in a standard ZIP format.
  • Withdraw Consent: You can opt out of analytics and optional data sharing from Privacy settings.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Upon account deletion, personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. Anonymized usage statistics may be retained for platform improvement.
